What I Do

As a dedicated Nursery School teacher, my life is rich with the joy of fostering young minds. At the heart of my vocation lies a passion for creativity, which I express through a plethora of crafts designed to engage, educate, and inspire the children in my care. Each unique piece serves a dual purpose - providing tactile, visual stimulation for little hands and eyes, while also teaching essential developmental skills like fine motor coordination, problem-solving, and imaginative thinking.

On 'My Work', you will find a gallery of my crafted creations and school activities, each carefully documented to offer parents a glimpse into our vibrant classroom. From handmade educational toys to interactive art pieces and learning aids, my craftwork represents the beauty of learning through play, and the limitless potential of our youngest learners.

Who I Am

Hello! I am a diligent and passionate professional, holding a BBA in Marketing from the Army Institute of Business Administration. In my career journey, I've had the opportunity to work at United Commercial Bank Ltd as an intern, and I am currently nurturing young minds as a teacher at Hello Kids International School.

My academic and professional achievements are not my only pride; I have also enjoyed success as an avid badminton player and have actively contributed to cultural and community initiatives. Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy singing, drawing, and crafting. Fluent in both Bangla and English, I am a disciplined and dynamic individual who is always eager to learn and grow.
